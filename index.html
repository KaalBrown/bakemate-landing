Why Only 10 Founding Partners?

We want to personally onboard every founding baker, help optimise your listings, provide launch support and build a strong marketplace from day one. Limiting the first intake allows us to give every partner the attention they deserve.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to join?

Founding Partners pay a discounted one-time setup fee of just $20 (normally $100), enjoy a reduced 10% commission rate for their first year, receive priority placement, and get personal onboarding.

Do I need a physical bakery?

No. Home bakers and bakery shops are welcome, provided they meet local food regulations.

When will BakeMate launch?

We're working behind the scenes to build Christchurch's best cake marketplace. Our customer launch will follow once we've completed onboarding our Founding Baker Partners and finished final platform testing.
